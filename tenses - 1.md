# Present Tenses

```mermaid
mindmap
  root((English Tenses Present))

    Simple Present
      Structure
        S - V1 s es - O
      Use
        Habits
        Routines
        General truths
      Example
        She plays tennis every Sunday

    Present Continuous
      Structure
        S - am is are - V ing - O
      Use
        Happening now
        Temporary actions
      Example
        She is playing tennis now

    Present Perfect
      Structure
        S - has have - V3 - O
      Use
        Completed actions
        Life experience
        Present result
      Example
        She has played tennis many times

    Present Perfect Continuous
      Structure
        S - has have been - V ing - O
      Use
        Started in past
        Continues now
      Example
        She has been playing for two hours

    Legend
      S Subject
      V1 Base Verb
      V3 Past Participle
      O Object
```

# Past Tenses


```mermaid
mindmap
  root((English Tenses Past))

    Simple Past
      Structure
        S - V2 - O
      Use
        Finished past action
      Example
        She played tennis yesterday

    Past Continuous
      Structure
        S - was were - V ing - O
      Use
        Action in progress in past
      Example
        She was playing at five PM

    Past Perfect
      Structure
        S - had - V3 - O
      Use
        Before another past action
      Example
        She had played before it rained

    Past Perfect Continuous
      Structure
        S - had been - V ing - O
      Use
        Duration before past point
      Example
        She had been playing for one hour

    Legend
      S Subject
      V2 Past Verb
      V3 Past Participle
      O Object
```
